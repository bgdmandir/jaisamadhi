Sain Gurdas ram Sahib Ji

The Divine Life of Baba Gurdas ram Sahib

Ghauspur (Sindh) Ka Har Bacha, Jawan Aur Boodha Us Waqt Aisa Mehsoos Kar Raha Tha Jaise Andheri Kaali Raatein Khatam Hone Wali Hain. Iski Wajah Yeh Thi Ke Sain Gareeb Das Sahib Ne Mukha (Khas) Logo Ko Ishara Diya Tha Ke Gurdani (Sain Gurdas Ram) Ko Gadi Par Bithaya Jaye.

Jab Unki Dastaar-Bandi (Gadi Par Bethne) Ka Din Kareeb Aaya, To Logo Mein Josh Barhta Gaya. Sakht Sardi Aur Sawari Ki Sahuliyat Na Hone Ke Bawajood, Log Apne Mehboob Ke Darshan Ke Liye Deewane Ho Rahe The. Gurdani Lal Ko Sirf 16 Saal Ki Umar Mein Ghauspur Darbar Ki Gadi Par Bithaya Gaya.

Maryada Ke Mutabiq, Mahraj Mohan Lal Ne Unhe Tilak Lagaya Aur Unhe Swami Gurdass Ram Sahib Ke Martabe Par Pohanchaya. Unke Gadi Par Bethte Hi Aisa Laga Jaise "Jungle Mein Mangal" Ho Gaya Ho Aur Khushi Ki Leher Daur Gayi.

Sain Gurdass Ram Sahib Ka Akhri Safar: Duniya Fani Hai Aur Jo Aya Hai Usne Jana Hai. Sain Gareeb Das Sahib Ke Mele Ke Mauqe Par, Sain Gurdass Ram Ne Pehle Hi Ishara De Diya Tha Ke "Ab Hum Sat-Khand (Parlok) Chale Jayenge".

Akhri Din: Sakht Garmi Ke Bawajood Unhone Darbar Sahib Ki Tameer Aur Sewa Jari Rakhi. Wafat Ki Tareekh: 22 April 1975 Ko Woh Amravati Ke Liye Rawana Hue. Raste Mein Unhone Kaafi Bhakton Se Mulaqat Ki Aur Mohabbat Ka Paigham Diya. Akhri Lamhat: Unhe Saans Lene Mein Takleef Hui. Sanatan Dharm Ke Mutabiq, Unhone Akhri Waqt Mein Tulsi Ke Patte Aur Shehad (Honey) Maanga, Jo Ke Khush-Naseeb Logo Ko Hi Naseeb Hota Hai.

Samadhi: 3 May 1975 Ko Unhe Usi Kamre Mein Samadhi Di Gayi Jiska Ishara Unhone Pehle Se Diya Tha.

Bachpan Aur Taleem: Sain Gurdass Ram Sahib 15 June 1931 Ko Baba Khajuram Ke Ghar Paida Hue. Unka Bachpan Ka Naam Gurdino Rakha Gaya. Ustad: Unhe Mazhabi Taleem Ke Liye Shikarpur Bheja Gaya, Jahan Sain Arjan Das Ne Unhe Parhaya.

Sewa: Woh Bachpan Se Hi Bahut Namre (Humble) The Aur Raat Ko Der Tak Parhne Ke Bawajood Subah Sawere Uth Kar Ibadat (Path Puja) Karte The. Amanat: Jab Partition Ke Waqt Log Ja Rahe The, To Unke Ustad Ne Bataya Ke Gurdani "Ghauspur Darbar Ki Amanat" Hain, Isliye Woh Wahin Rahe.



Sain Gurdas Ram Sahib Jan Ka Phale Naam Bhai Gurdino Tha. Sain Gurdas Ram Sahib Jan Ki Mata Ka Naam Takhtan Bai Aur Pita Ka Naam Baba Gaju Ram Tha.

Sain Gurdas Ram Sahib Jan 15th June 1931 Me Garhi Tegho, Zilla Shikarpur Me Paida Hue. Unko Ek Bhai Tha Jiska Naam Dada Fateh Chand Tha Jo Umar Me Sain Jan Se Barhe The.

Sain Gurdas Ram Sahib Jan Ke Vidya Guru Ka Naam Mahraj Mohan Lal Tha. Sain Gurdas Ram Sahib Jan Ko 16 Saalon Ki Umar Me 1947 Me Gadi Sahib Pe Bithaya Gaya. Sain Gurdas Ram Sahib Jan 28 Saal Gadi Sahib Par Baithe.

English Version

At that time, every child, youth, and elder of Ghauspur felt as if their dark nights were ending. This was because Sain Gareeb Das Sahib had instructed the key devotees to seat Gurdani (Sain Gurdas Ram) on the Gadi.

As the day of his coronation approached, excitement grew among the devotees. Despite harsh winter and lack of transportation, people were eager to witness their beloved. Gurdani Lal was seated on the Ghauspur Darbar Gadi at the age of only 16.

According to tradition, Mahraj Mohan Lal applied tilak and elevated him to the status of Swami Gurdass Ram Sahib. The moment he was seated on the Gadi, it felt as if joy had spread through the entire region.

Final Journey: The world is transient, and everyone must depart. On the occasion of Sain Gareeb Das Sahib’s gathering, Sain Gurdas Ram indicated that it was time to proceed to Sat-Khand (the divine abode).

Last Day: Despite the intense heat, he continued the construction and service at the Darbar Sahib. Date of Passing: 22 April 1975, en route to Amravati. Along the way, he met many devotees and shared messages of love. His final moments: He experienced difficulty in breathing. According to Sanatan tradition, he requested Tulsi leaves and honey, a blessing granted to only the fortunate.

Samadhi: On 3 May 1975, he was interred in the same chamber he had indicated beforehand.

Childhood and Education: Born on 15 June 1931 to Baba Khajuram, his childhood name was Gurdino. For religious education, he was sent to Shikarpur, where Sain Arjan Das taught him.

Service: From childhood, he was humble and devoted. Despite studying late at night, he would wake early for prayers and meditation. Responsibility: During the Partition, when others were leaving, his teacher informed him that Gurdani is the “trust of Ghauspur Darbar,” so he remained there.



Sain Gurdas Ram Sahib Ji was initially named Bhai Gurdino. His mother was Takhtan Bai and his father was Baba Gaju Ram.

He was born on 15th June 1931 in Garhi Tegho, District Shikarpur, and had an elder brother named Dada Fateh Chand. His spiritual education was guided by Mahraj Mohan Lal.

At the age of 16, in 1947, he was seated on the spiritual seat (Gadi) at Ghauspur Darbar. By the age of 28, he fully assumed the responsibilities of the seat.
