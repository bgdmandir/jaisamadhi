About Jai Samadhi

Established approximately 200 years ago, this sacred sanctuary has stood as a beacon of hope and faith through centuries. It features an ancient sacred well whose waters are believed to have healing properties for pilgrims.

While the temple has been modernized to accommodate the growing number of devotees, it remains a pillar of faith under the devoted sevadari of Dada Hikmat Rai, continuing the legacy of service and spirituality.

Jai Samadhi is not only a place of worship but a spiritual movement rooted in devotion, unity, and divine remembrance. The sacred teachings of the five revered Gurus guide devotees toward peace, humility, and inner awakening.

Through bhajans, seva, satsang, and sacred traditions, Jai Samadhi continues to inspire thousands of followers across Pakistan and around the world.

The temple remains a symbol of faith, spiritual discipline, and timeless heritage.
